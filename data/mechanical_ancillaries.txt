Mechanical Ancillaries

Advanced Martial 
Requirements: 
+3 Physical
+100 Battle
-Fledgling Martial Ancillary
-At least 6 Martial Prowess Abilities Unlocked

You gain +2 AP per round strictly for the purpose of moving or making attacks or reactions with martial prowess abilities. 

Advanced Psion
Requirements: 
+2 Mental
-Fledgling Psion Ancillary
+80 Psionic Technique

Unlock Mental attribute 2 Point Techniques you have access to. These Techniques costs 1 Energy.

Athletic 
Requirements: 
+3 Physical 
+50 Feat of Strength

-1 energy/per round costs if applicable to equipped shields and armors, minimum 0 energy per round, and you can ignore exhaustion the first time it is incurred, once per moonface

Demigod
Requirements: 
-Pontifex Maximus Ancillary
+4 Physical, Mental, or Will 

1, 3, 7, 15, and 25 cost Divine Interventions with your Deity now cost energy instead of Deity Relationship points. 

Diviner Apprentice 
Requirements: 
-Clergy background
-Member of Thairin Church or Thairin Race
+1 Spiritual
+50 Worship and Divine Intervention

You choose a specific Deity to be permanently dedicated to. With this Deity, you gain a passive spiritual attribute amount of deity relationship points. Add your character tier to your deity relationship point caps.

Energetic 
Requirements: 
+3 Physical 

You begin the game with +10% energy at character creation, and gain +10% every level-up. You may only take this ancillary once. 

Epic Martial 
Requirements: 
+5 Physical
+200 Battle
-Heroic Martial Ancillary
-At least 12 Martial Prowess Abilities unlocked and 2 Martial Prowess categories with all Martial Prowess Abilities unlocked
-1 Martial Prowess category with more points invested than required to unlock all abilities 

You gain +3 AP per round strictly for the purpose of moving or making attacks or reactions with martial prowess abilities. When you successfully hit with a martial prowess ability, it is automatically a wicked critical.

Legendary Martial 
Requirements: 
+6 Physical
+250 Battle 
-Epic Martial Ancillary
-At least 15 Martial Prowess Abilities unlocked
-1 MP where you have double the points invested to unlock all MP abilities in the category. 

You gain +3 AP per round strictly for the purpose of moving or making attacks or reactions with martial prowess abilities. Whenever you successfully hit with a martial prowess ability, it is automatically a vicious critical.

Mythic Martial 
Requirements: 
-Legendary Martial Ancillary
-20 Martial Prowess Abilities unlocked, at least 2 Martial Prowess categories where you have double the points to unlock all abilities in the MP category. 

You gain +3 AP per round strictly for the purpose of moving or making attacks or reactions with martial prowess abilities. Whenever you successfully hit an opponent with a martial prowess ability, it is a brutal critical.

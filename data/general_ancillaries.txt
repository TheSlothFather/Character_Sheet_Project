+4 Spiritual
+1 Will

Purple: All prior Fireborn abilities may now be used to deal mental damage instead. All creatures with at least -1 Mental now take additional damage equal to spiritual attribute.

Green: All prior Fireborn abilities may now be used to deal poison damage and poison instead of burn. All beasts and sentient plants take additional damage equal to spiritual attribute. 

Black: All prior Fireborn abilities may now be used to deal necrosis damage and necrotize instead of burn. Undead take additional damage equal to spiritual attribute.
